**A powerful Win+V alternative. Manage your clipboard history with rich format support (files, images, text) and instantly prevent data loss during RDP disconnects. 100% offline, secure, and lightweight.**

### 🛑 The RDP "Anchor" You Need
Have you ever copied a crucial snippet of code, an image, or a file on a remote server, only for your RDP session to suddenly freeze or drop? You restart the connection, go to paste locally, and... nothing. The clipboard is empty. 

**RDP Clipboard Anchor** was born to solve this exact pain point. Running safely on your local machine, it instantly and automatically caches your cross-session copy events. When your Remote Desktop (RDP) connection hangs or crashes, your copied data is safely "anchored" locally, ready to be pasted or dragged anywhere.

### 🚀 A Superior Local Clipboard Manager
Beyond saving your remote sessions, it is a powerhouse replacement for the native Windows clipboard history (Win+V):
* **Rich Format Support:** Handles complex data types, images, and files far better than standard text-only tools.
* **Seamless Drag & Drop:** Drag items straight from your history directly into other applications or folders.
* **Smart Cache Management:** Keep your storage optimized with customizable quotas and automated, intelligent cleanup logic.
* **Quick Actions:** Double-click items to instantly open or execute them.
* **Native Experience:** A clean, unobtrusive WinUI 3 interface that perfectly matches Windows 11.

### 🛡️ 100% Offline & Secure
Your clipboard often contains highly sensitive information. This application operates entirely locally with **zero internet access and zero telemetry**. It intelligently respects your privacy by automatically ignoring temporary clipboard events from major password managers (like 1Password). What you copy stays strictly under your control.
